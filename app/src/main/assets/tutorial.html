How to Play

Green balls are your soldiers.

Red balls are enemies.

Each enemy killed gives +3 gold.

You can buy 1 soldier for 5 gold.

To buy, just tap on the plus!

Every round, 2 more enemies arrive.

But remember...

If you recruit too many soldiers,

they may feel unimportant and stop following you.

Too many soldiers can lose morale.

Also be careful:

If enemy numbers grow 5 more than yours,

they will unite and attack you directly!

Think wisely. Power needs balance and timing.

You can also swipe down to reset the game.
